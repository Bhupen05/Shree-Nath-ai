## 🎓 KEY CONCEPTS YOU NEED

### **The Core Business Logic** (In: CORE_FEATURES_SYSTEM_DESIGN.md)
1. **Stock Management** - Track inventory by location (Room/Cabinet/Section)
2. **Bills** - Create bills that auto-reduce stock
3. **Customers** - Manage customer relationships & history
4. **Reminders** - Send SMS/Email/WhatsApp for due bills
5. **Voice AI** - Answer calls about stock availability

### **The Critical Path** (In: IMMEDIATE_ACTION_PLAN.md)
1. **Week 1-2: Stock** (foundation everything depends on)
2. **Week 3-4: Bills** (links stock to revenue)
3. **Week 5: Customers** (links bills to customers)
4. **Week 6-8: Reminders + Voice** (increases engagement)

### **The Most Tricky Part** (In: PROJECT_MANAGER_SUMMARY.md)
- **Bill-Stock Integration** - Bills must reduce stock atomically (no double-deductions)
- **Stock Accuracy** - Must be 99.5%+ accurate
- **Concurrent Requests** - Multiple bills reducing same stock simultaneously
